Loaded Baked Potatoe Soup

Description

An easy and delicious creamy potatoe soup that can be made in just one pot! Loaded with bacon and creamy potatoe goodness, this is a household comfort food

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 5 russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot over medium heat. Add bacon and cook until brown and crispy, about 6-8 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate; set aside.
  2. Melt butter in the pot. Add onion, and cook, stirring frequently, until onions have become translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Stir in garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Whisk in flour until lightly browned, about 1 minute.
  4. Gradually whisk in chicken broth and cook, whisking constantly, until incorporated, about 1-2 minutes. Stir in potatoes.
  5. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender, about 12-15 minutes.
  6. Stir in heavy cream and sour cream until heated through, about 1-2 minutes; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  7. If the soup is too thick, add more chicken broth as needed until desired consistency is reached.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with bacon, cheese, and green onions, if desired.
